Easy Zucchini Slice Recipe

The zucchini slice is my all-time favorite weeknight dinner, and is a great recipe for weeknight dinners when you're short on time

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Bacon
  • Broccoli
  • Capsicum
  • Spinach
  • Zucchini

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180 celsius
  2. Beat up your eggs in a bowl
  3. Cook your veggies and bacon
  4. Grate zucchini
  5. Grate cheese
  6. Put all ingredients in a dish, make sure it's mixed evenly
  7. Cook for 40 minutes
