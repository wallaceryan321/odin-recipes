Stir Fry Recipe

The stir fry is a delicious, easy and flexible weeknight dinner

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Oil
  • Noodles
  • Veggies
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil on pan
  2. Cook beef
  3. Put the rest of the veggies in
  4. Cook noodles according to package instructions
  5. Put noodles in the pan
  6. Put soy sauce and oyster sauce in
